Roast chicken

A sumptuous recipe to make roast chicken!

Roast chicken is well-suited to be your main dish for any feast.

Ingredients:

  • A chicken
  • White wine
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic cloves
  • Mace
  • Two egg yolks

Instructions:

  1. Put chicken in a roasting pan with wine, broth, and garlic cloves.
  2. Bake in the oven for an hour.
  3. Baste with the broth, turn pan in oven.
  4. Take from the oven, pour juices into saucepan, and separate fat.
  5. Add yolks to thicken and serve.
