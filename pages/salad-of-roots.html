Salad of Roots

A delcious salad composed of root vegetables.

The salad of roots is made from many fine vegetables. It can be enjoyed on its own, ot accompany a more hearty main dish.

Ingredients:

  • Carrots
  • Parsnips
  • Turnips
  • Olive Oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Wash and peel the carrots, parsnips, and turnips.
  2. Cut the vegetables into pieces, then roast until tender.
  3. Mix the ingredients together.
  4. Dress with oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
